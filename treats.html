Treats, Treats, Treats!

You've made it to the treat section. Woohoo! These are a few recipes that Arya absolutely loves! Now, Arya does not own these recipes. Arya is simply providing a recommended list with links to baking blogs. Arya divided up her recommendations into two sections:

In Arya's Favorites, Arya recommends three scrumptious and unique options! There's lots of variety in flavors that she's sure your pup will love.

In Healthy Options, Arya recommends three yummy health concious options if your pup is watching their waistline (or if you as a pet parent are watching your pet's waistline). No shame if you gotta stick to a diet.

Want more? Check out these recipes:

  • Fluent Woof has a list of 105 treat recipes! They make it easy, grouping options into different categories for you to explore quickly.
  • Humane Society of Southern Arizona has 6 tasty recipies that all are peanut butter based. Yumm, right?!!
  • Taste of Home has a list of 9 truly unique treat recipes ranging from tiny doggie donuts to doggie pretzels. Go take a look!
