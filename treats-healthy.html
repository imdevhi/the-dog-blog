  • Pook's Pantry's Peanut Butter & Banana

    This healthy recipe is made from old fashioned oats (not quick oats), banana, peanut butter, egg, and optional flour. It takes about 23 minutes total with prep and bake time. Your pup will definitely linger around the oven while these are baking. The banana and peanut butter smell delicious!

  • Belly Full's Carrot Cake

    These treats are made with applesauce, almond butter or peanut butter, eggs, oat flour, grated carrots, and unsweetened coconut flakes. It's a pretty quick recipe only requiring 30 minutes in total for prep and bake time. You'll get roughly 40 treats from a single batch. This is another good option for the holiday season! Yum!

  • Pet Lifestyle Guru's Butternut Squash

    This unique recipe is make from butternut squash, peanut butter, flaxseed, ground oats, and cinnamon. It's another quick recipe that only takes 30 minutes for prep and bake time. Why's butternut squash such a great option for your pup? It's got vitamin C, B6, fiber, and potassium!
