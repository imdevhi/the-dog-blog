  • All Recipes's Peanut Butter & Pumpkin

    This yummy recipe is made from whole wheat flour, eggs, canned pumpkin, peanut butter, salt, and ground cinnamon. It makes about 25 treats per batch and overall takes about an hour and 40 minutes to prep and bake. This is a great recipe for treats during the fall season.

  • 365 Days of Baking's Chicken & Cheese

    These delcious treats are made with ground chicken, mozzarella cheese, parsley, flaxseed, eggs, whole wheat flour, and vegetable oil. This recipe takes about 40 minutes in total to prep and bake. A single batch makes about 53 treats. It's easiest to shape these guys in a little ball. They can be tricky when using cookie cutters.

  • Tasty Yummies' Salmon Pumpkin

    This truly delicious recipe is made from coconut flour, canned wild caught salmon, coconut oil, pumpkin puree, eggs, and parsley. It is grain-free, gluten-free, dairy-free, nut-free, and paleo. These treats take about 35 minutes in total to prep and bake and requires a food processor to blend all the ingredients together. Om nom nom.
